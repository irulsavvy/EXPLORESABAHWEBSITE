TANJUNG ARU

JUST VIBING

Tanjung Aru Beach, located just a short drive from Kota Kinabalu city, is one of Sabah’s most iconic coastal destinations. This long stretch of soft sand and calm sea is especially famous for its breathtaking sunsets, drawing locals and tourists every evening to witness the golden hues as the sun dips below the horizon. The beach is lined with palm trees and open spaces, creating a perfect spot for relaxation, family picnics, or a leisurely walk.

In addition to its natural beauty, Tanjung Aru is also a hub for local street food and vibrant culture. Food stalls near the beach offer delicious snacks such as satay, grilled seafood, coconut shakes, and mango desserts. Visitors can also enjoy watching planes land at the nearby airport — a unique experience as aircraft fly low right over the beach, perfect for photography lovers.

Whether you're visiting for a quiet sunset, a taste of local flavors, or to simply enjoy the sea breeze, Tanjung Aru delivers a memorable coastal experience. It’s easily accessible by car, Grab, or public bus, making it a convenient stop for anyone exploring Kota Kinabalu and the nearby attractions.

Tanjung Aru Beach is a popular coastal destination near Kota Kinabalu, Sabah, known for its stunning sunsets, soft sands, and local food stalls. Just minutes from the city, it offers a relaxing atmosphere perfect for evening strolls, family outings, and scenic views of planes landing nearby. With its unique blend of natural beauty and cultural charm, Tanjung Aru is a must-visit spot for travelers exploring Sabah.
