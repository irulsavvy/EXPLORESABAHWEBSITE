KUNDASANG

RELAX YOUR MIND

Kundasang is a small town located in the district of Ranau, Sabah, Malaysia. Situated at an altitude of over 1,900 meters above sea level, it is a highland destination known for its cool weather, breathtaking views of Mount Kinabalu, and peaceful atmosphere far from the hustle and bustle of city life. Located just about 6 km from Kinabalu National Park, Kundasang serves as a popular stopover for tourists who want to admire or climb the iconic Mount Kinabalu. Its natural beauty, lush green landscape, and rural charm make it one of the most beloved travel spots in Sabah.

Kundasang is an ideal getaway for anyone seeking fresh mountain air, stunning natural landscapes, and a slower pace of life. With its breathtaking views, cultural richness, and unique attractions, Kundasang stands out as one of the most beautiful and peaceful travel destinations in Sabah and Malaysia as a whole.

Desa Dairy Farm terletak di Kundasang, Sabah dan dikenali sebagai “New Zealand versi Malaysia” kerana cuaca sejuk dan pemandangan indah berlatarbelakangkan Gunung Kinabalu. Ia merupakan ladang tenusu moden yang menempatkan lembu baka Friesian-Holstein dari New Zealand. Pengunjung boleh melihat proses pemerahan susu, memberi makan haiwan, serta mencuba produk tenusu segar seperti susu pelbagai perisa, yogurt dan ais krim. Ladang ini sesuai untuk lawatan keluarga, pelajar dan pelancong yang ingin menikmati suasana tanah tinggi yang nyaman. Terletak hanya beberapa kilometer dari pekan Kundasang, Desa Dairy Farm merupakan salah satu tarikan utama dalam sektor agro-pelancongan Sabah dan destinasi wajib singgah bagi pengunjung ke kawasan ini.

Sosodikon Hill is a small hill located near Desa Dairy Farm in Kundasang, Sabah. It offers an easy 10–15 minute hike, making it suitable for all ages. From the top, visitors can enjoy stunning 360-degree views, including Mount Kinabalu, Kundasang Valley, and surrounding farmlands. The hill is a popular sunrise spot, with a wooden viewing platform perfect for photography. The best time to visit is before 8 AM to avoid cloud cover. A small entrance fee is charged (RM5 for local adults), and basic facilities like parking are available nearby. With its calm atmosphere, scenic views, and easy access, Sosodikon Hill is a must-visit destination for nature lovers and casual hikers exploring Kundasang.

Kundasang is a serene highland town located in the district of Ranau, Sabah, Malaysia. Nestled at the foothills of Mount Kinabalu, it is renowned for its cool climate, stunning natural landscapes, and panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. Often referred to as the “New Zealand of Borneo,” Kundasang attracts nature lovers, photographers, and travelers seeking a peaceful escape from city life. Its rolling green hills, vegetable farms, and charming homestays offer a unique rural experience set against the dramatic backdrop of Southeast Asia's tallest peak. Aside from its scenic beauty, Kundasang holds historical and cultural significance. It is home to the Kundasang War Memorial, built to honor the Australian and British prisoners of war who died during the Sandakan Death Marches in World War II. The town is also close to Kinabalu Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, making it a popular base for climbers and eco-tourists. With its mix of natural charm, historical depth, and local hospitality, Kundasang continues to be a must-visit gem in Sabah’s travel landscape.
